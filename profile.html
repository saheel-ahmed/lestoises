About Dr. Alisha Grant

Dr. Alisha Grant is a licensed clinical psychologist with over a decade of experience helping individuals navigate life's challenges. She specializes in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and mindfulness-based approaches to treat anxiety, depression, and relationship issues. Dr. Grant believes in a collaborative, empathetic approach, creating a safe and non-judgmental space for clients to explore their thoughts and feelings. Her goal is to empower clients with practical tools to foster resilience, self-awareness, and lasting positive change.
